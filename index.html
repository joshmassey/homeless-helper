About

Homelessness is a widespread epidemic across the nation as well as throughout the rest of the world. It is especially an issue in large, dense cities such as New York and Baltimore. With Live Better, we aim to provide some degree of relief and support to those that are struggling to attain basic necessities daily. By texting their area ID to our number, we will send the help-seeker locations of the closest shelters and places where they can receive aid.
